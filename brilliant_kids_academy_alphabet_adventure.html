🎓 Teachers & Parents Guide

Welcome to Brilliant Kids Academy's interactive alphabet adventure! Here's how to play and practice:

  • Alphabet Explorer: Let your child tap letters. Choose tabs to toggle matching categories. Tap cards to repeat speech.
  • Match the Letter: A multiple-choice quiz designed to strengthen phonics and alphabet sound connections.
  • Listen & Tap: Tests sound recognition. Listen to the letter voice then tap the appropriate alphabet card.
  • Learn by Category: Filters words by cute groups to enrich child's early vocabulary.

💡 Tip: Make sure your system's text-to-speech volume is turned up to hear correct pronunciations!
